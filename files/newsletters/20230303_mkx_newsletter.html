Intentions invites the listener on a journey of spiritual transformation through sound; it's an immersive journey to explore and expand into new states of consciousness and being. Born out of a life-changing spiritual experience, ANNA's new sound emerged from her deep dive into meditation and shamanic healing practices, and it is beautiful!

Alongside the announcement, the Brazilian-born, Lisbon-based DJ and producer shares the record's first single, Receiving, a lulling and featherlight offering designed for surrendering to what is and welcoming what appears, featuring ambient pioneer and legend, Laraaji.

 

Intentions is the result of ANNA's self-realisation journey - opening her mind and forever altering her creative process and worldly outlook. The result sees her trading in her tougher, club-driven production for a world of sound healing and ambient sensibilities. The album comes with a special listening guide inviting the audience to set an intention for each track, which has been ordered and equipped with its own specific purpose on the album.

"Intentions captures the purest longing for the universal power of love. It's borne out of a deep well of love that exists inside of me, and an enduring appreciation for all that we call 'life'" - ANNA

ANNA discovered a passion for classical music and ambient after opening herself up to a more contemplative and love-filled way of life. Her journey for this album started with experimentations with sounds and frequencies as a tool for meditation and contemplation. Using pianos and flutes and sound healing techniques, she'd listen to these works - these soundscapes for self-realisation - while exploring the world.
